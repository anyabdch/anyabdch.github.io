Hi! Welcome to my site

The Fun Stuff

I'm a sophomore at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Human Development. But I'm getting ahead of myself! I'm originally from Santa Monica, CA. I play water polo and I love hiking, swimming, a good book, and travel. I'm bilingual! I'm fluent in Spanish, and proficient in Italian and French. Before coming to college, I devoted a large portion of my time to community service projects. I've worked on and ran various endeavors including asylum case interpretation, COVID-19 grocery deliveries, access to feminine hygiene products at my high school, and more.

My CS Background

My first quarter at Northwestern I took the introductory CS class in Python. I fell in love with the subject and knew that I had found my path. By the end of my first month I had declared as a CS major, and by the end of my first year I had a job TAing for the department. Programming has become my favorite passtime and the perfect fuel for my curiosity. I've taken classes in various languages, including Python, C, and C++, as well as in exploring data structures and algorithms, web development, and mathematical theory in CS. In my (limited) free time, I've been teaching myself Django, Flask, and other frameworks to build personal projects such as the ones back on the home page. To date Python remains my favorite language, but I'm also enjoying exploring front-end endeavors (such as this one!)

My Aspirations

Community service is still incredibly important to me. At the start of my sophomore year I declared a second major in Human Development. These classes have helped me gain a more nuanced perspective of the world around me and understand where work is needed. As I build my CS skills, I'm always thinking about how I can use them to help the people around me. I'm currently developing an app to help local cafes and residents work together to reduce food waste and food injustice in Evanston. My goal, when I graduate, is to work in a setting where what I produce goes to efforts towards social justice, community service, and bettering the world we live in.
